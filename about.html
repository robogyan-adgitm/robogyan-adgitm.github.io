About Us

Welcome to Robogyan

We are a student-run club of ADGITM focused on providing knowledge and experience in robotics and automation. Robotics deals with the design, construction, operation, and use of robots and computer systems for their control, sensory feedback, and information processing. We create open software and hardware platforms for robotics.

Our Mission

  • To promote engineering and STEM into the community through presentations and demonstrations
  • Assist students in mastering new skills that can be helpful for resumes and opportunities.
  • Host hackathons or like what industry visits to provide exposure to students and show them the new industry trends.
  • Guide students on how to build unique robots and develop new skills along the way
  • Help students to fill the gap between implementation of a problem with practical approach and guide them on how to solve real world problems.

We are looking for new members of any major to join our group highly driven individuals who want to make a difference in the ever-advancing field of Robotics. Currently, we are participating in competitions and have aspirations of beginning larger robotics projects.
Be a part of our communinty and improve your skills in the field of robotics . If you have the zeal to do something different from others and want to guide others too then join us and aid students in the best way possible.
