About Us

Welcome to Robogyan

We are a student-run club of ADGITMfocused on providing knowledge and experience in robotics and automation. Robotics deals with the design, construction, operation, and use of robots and computer systems for their control, sensory feedback, and information processing. We create open software and hardware platforms for robotics

Our Mission

  • To promote engineering and STEM into the community through presentations and demonstrations
  • Help students to gain many skills that can be helpful for resumes and opportunities
  • Hosts different companies to show students different industry trends
  • Teaches students how to build unique robots and learn new skills along the way
  • Help student to fill gap between implementing problem pratically and how to approach them by guiding them to how to solve real world problems

We are always looking for new members of any major to join our group of motivated individuals who want to make a difference in the ever-advancing field of robotics. Currently, we participate in competitions and have aspirations of beginning larger robotics projects.
Be our part and improve your skills in the field of robotics . If you have zeal to do something different from other and want to guide other students also you can join our team and help student in any major .
